About The Mentor

Saleem Ahmed Sir Physics Wallah

Biography

Saleem Ahmed Sir, well known as “Saleem Bhaiya” among the students in Kota, is a highly respected figure in the field of education. He completed his B.Tech in Electronics and Computing from NIT Trichy and has amassed over 9 years of teaching experience in various renowned institutes in Kota. Currently associated with Physics Wallah, Saleem Sir plays a crucial role in imparting knowledge and expertise in the subject of Physics. His impact is far-reaching, as he has mentored over 10,000 students through offline batches and has reached a vast online audience comprising lakhs of students via platforms like Physics Wallah and ISAC.
