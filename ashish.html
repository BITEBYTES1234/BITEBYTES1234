About The Mentor

Ashish Agarwal

Biography

Ashish Agarwal Sir is one of the highly experienced faculty members at Physics Wallah, with an impressive teaching career spanning over 26 years. Previously, he served as the Head of the Department (HOD) of Mathematics at Bansal Kota, where he dedicated 12-13 years to guiding and mentoring students. Ashish Aggarwal Sir holds a Gold Medalist distinction in his Bachelor’s MBA program, having graduated from IMT Ghaziabad. Renowned as the “King of Mathematics” Ashish Agarwal Sir has played a pivotal role in mentoring top-ranked students, including those who achieved impressive All India Ranks (AIR) such as 1, 4, 5, 7, and 8. Currently, he imparts his expertise in Mathematics at the PW platform, continuing to inspire and guide students.
