Tarun Khandelwal Physics Wallah

Biography

Tarun Khandelwal Sir is a brilliant person who cracked India’s most difficult exam called JEE Advanced. He got an AIR 289 in JEE Advanced and studied engineering at IIT Delhi. Tarun Sir has been teaching for 12 years and helping students from different parts of the country. He has been teaching Math at PW for over a year now. Throughout his career, Tarun Sir has taught and guided more than 20,000 students, and this number keeps growing. Some of his students even got really good ranks like 21, 32, 36, 54, 86, 105, and many more. Before joining Physics Wallah, Tarun Khandelwal worked as the head of operations and math teacher at PACE Institute for 10 years. Now, he is an amazing and enthusiastic educator at Physics Wallah.
